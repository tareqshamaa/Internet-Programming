Self Driving Car

A self-driving car, also known as an autonomous vehicle (AV or auto), driverless car, or robotic car (robo-car),is a vehicle that is capable of sensing its environment and moving safely with little or no human input.The future of this technology may have an impact on multiple industries and other circumstances. Self-driving cars combine a variety of sensors to perceive their surroundings, such as radar, lidar, sonar, GPS, odometry and inertial measurement units. Advanced control systems interpret sensory information to identify appropriate navigation paths, as well as obstacles and relevant signage.
