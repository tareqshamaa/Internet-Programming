Features and Usage

Features

  1. Autonomous cars create and maintain a map of their surroundings based on a variety of sensors situated in different parts of the vehicle.
  2. Radar sensors monitor the position of nearby vehicles.
  3. Video cameras detect traffic lights, read road signs, track other vehicles, and look for pedestrians.
  4. Replace driver assistance with automated safety features to navigate roads.
  5. Adaptive Cruise Control
  6. Autonomous Emergency Braking (AEB).
